About

EthCC 2019 (Ethereum Community Conference) was the second edition of a series of conferences and workshops that bring together and strengthen the international Ethereum community. EthCC took place in Paris, on March 5th, 6th and 7th. EthCC makes a point of being inclusive. It is non-profit and the tickets are sold at cost price. Everybody is welcome, tech-savy neophytes and experts, developers and non-techs, blue and white collars, students and researchers ...

EthCC 2019 was the place to be for:

  • Meeting and listening to over 250 international speakers
  • Catching up with the latest developments on Ethereum
  • Having access to multiple tracks : Decentralized Finance, Scalability, Web 3.0, from Zero to Hero, ...
  • Learning new skills through workshops for developers & researchers
  • Networking with passionate forethinkers
